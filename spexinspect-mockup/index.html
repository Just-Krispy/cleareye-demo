No more typing reports for hours.

The workflow is built around Bobby's number-one pain point: finish the walkthrough, stop the inspection, and leave with the report already assembled.

1. Capture during walkthrough

Meta Ray-Ban photos auto-send to the AI as the inspector moves through the house.

2. Coach after each photo

SpexInspect voices practical prompts like "check under the sink" when moisture staining or missing context is detected.

3. Stop and generate

Type !inspect stop and the PDF report is generated automatically with photos, findings, categories, and cost estimates.

DJI Mavic T3 thermal roof

Thermal roof scans surface moisture and heat-loss patterns that are invisible to the naked eye.

  • Moisture anomaly flags
  • Heat-loss evidence
  • Roofing report placement

Sewer scope cam

Footage is analyzed for root intrusion, cracks, and belly or sag conditions in the pipe.

  • Root intrusion detection
  • Crack and separation flags
  • Belly/sag timeline notes

HVAC nameplate decoder

Snap a nameplate and the AI extracts model, serial, manufacture date, age, tonnage, SEER, refrigerant type such as R-22, R-410A, or R-32, service life, and replacement cost.

Cost estimates

Repair and replacement ranges are attached to defects so buyers and agents can negotiate with actual numbers.
